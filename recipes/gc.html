Grilled cheese

Grilled cheese cut in half

I will tell you the ingrediants and how to make a nice grilled cheese.

Lets start with the ingrediants.

  • two slices of bread
  • two slices of american cheese
  • 2 tbls of butter

Next i will tell you how to make it all.

  1. add the butter to the pan which should be over a heat of medium
  2. add the two slices of bread to the pan
  3. add one slice of cheese to each slice of bread
  4. let sit for about 5-7 minutes then take off pan when bread is golden and cheese is melted
