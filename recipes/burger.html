Burger recipe

Double cheese burger with toppings

This cheeseburger in the image above is a double cheese burger with some toppings. I will go through what you need to make the burger.

Here are the ingredients to be able to make a double cheeseburger like the one above.

  • two 1/4 pound patties
  • one onion
  • one tomato
  • two slices of american cheese
  • sliced pickles
  • buns

Now that we have our ingredients lets get to making our burger now.

  1. start with seasoning both your patties and heat up your pan with medium to high heat
  2. after your done seasoning throw the patties onto the pan and start to cut up your tomatos and onions
  3. cook for about 7-10 minutes add cheese in the las minute or two, then check the internal tempature and take them off the pan
  4. one burgers are on the buns start to add your sliced onions and tomatos to the patties and finish it off with some ketchup and mustard if you like.
