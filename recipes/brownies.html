How to make brownies

Brownies stacked on each other

i will tell you what you need for brownies and how to make them.

Here is what you will need to make brownies.

  • 3 tbls of water
  • 1/2 cup of vegatable oil
  • two eggs
  • one box of brownie mix
  • one baking pan

now here is how you make the brownies

  1. put brownie mix in the bowl then start adding your vegtable oil and water
  2. mix all ingrediants till you get a thick and goey mix
  3. pour the mix into your baking pan and preheat oven to 425 degrees
  4. once its heated put pan into the oven and leave for 35 minutes
  5. take out and make sure inside is cooked
